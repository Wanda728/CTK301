Who are Snoopy and Woodstock?

Snoopy is a loyal, imaginative, and good-natured beagle who is prone to imagining fantasy lives, including being an author, a college student known as "Joe Cool", an attorney, and a World War I flying ace. Woodstock is a yellow bird who is best known as Snoopy's closest friend and sidekick. Woodstock first appeared in the comic strip in 1967 and quickly became a beloved character among readers.
