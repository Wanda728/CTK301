The #1 song on my birthday

Beyoncé - Crazy In Love ft. JAY Z

On July 12, 2003, Beyoncé's "Crazy In Love" featuring Jay Z hit No.1 on the Billboard Hot 100. The lead single on Beyoncé's debut solo studio album, “Dangerously in Love,” went on to win two Grammys. "Crazy in Love" continued to dominate the charts, spending eight consecutive weeks at number one on the Hot 100, making it Beyoncé's first number one single in her solo career. My birthday is July 28, 2003, making this hit the top song that came out around my birthday.
