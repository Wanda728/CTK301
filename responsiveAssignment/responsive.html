Happy Birthday Homelander!

Season 4

The Boys Season 4 finally has a release date: June 13, 2024. The hit Prime Video anti-superhero show based on the comic by Garth Ennis and Darick Robertson returns with three episodes on June 13, followed by a new episode each week ending with a season finale on July 18. That makes for a Season 4 total of eight episodes. Apparently it’s Homelander’s birthday today, and to celebrate Prime Video also revealed new artwork for the fourth season. Happy birthday, Homelander!
